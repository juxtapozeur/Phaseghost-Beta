# Phaseghost

### Automatically turn your music and media into professional promotional videos.

Phaseghost is a macOS application built specifically for musicians, bands, DJs, producers, photographers, and content creators who want to create eye-catching promotional reels in minutes instead of spending hours editing video.

Unlike AI video generators, Phaseghost only uses **your own media**. Simply choose a song, point the app to a folder containing your videos, GIFs, or photos, and Phaseghost automatically generates multiple unique promotional videos optimized for social media.

---

## Features

* Automatic promotional reel generation
* Vertical, Square, and Landscape output formats
* Support for:

  * MP4
  * MOV
  * GIF
  * JPG
  * PNG
  * HEIC
* Randomized clip selection
* Custom logo overlays
* Title and subtitle overlays
* VHS visual treatment
* Black & White visual treatment
* Live preview monitor
* Generate multiple unique variations from the same media library

---

## Why Phaseghost?

Creating promotional videos for every new music release can take hours.

Every release requires:

* Finding usable clips
* Cutting and arranging footage
* Resizing for different social platforms
* Adding titles and branding
* Exporting multiple versions

Phaseghost automates those repetitive tasks while allowing you to retain complete creative control over your own media.

## Installation

1. Download the latest Phaseghost release.
2. Extract the ZIP file.
3. Move **Phaseghost.app** into your **Applications** folder.
4. Launch the application.

Because this is currently an unsigned beta application, macOS may block the first launch.

If that happens:

1. Control-click (or right-click) **Phaseghost.app**
2. Select **Open**
3. Click **Open** again when prompted

After the first launch, Phaseghost will open normally.

---

## Quick Start

1. Select your audio file.
2. Choose a folder containing your videos, GIFs, or images.
3. Add an optional logo, title, and subtitle.
4. Select your desired output format.
5. Click **Render Variations**.

Phaseghost automatically creates multiple promotional videos using your media.
